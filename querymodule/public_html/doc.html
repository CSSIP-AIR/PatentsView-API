Why Explore Patent Data?

Welcome to the prototype PatentsView API, supported by the US Patent and Trademark Office. The USPTO is the federal agency for granting US patents and registering trademarks. The US patenting system encourages innovators and entrepreneurs worldwide to publish their inventions for worldwide use and adoption.

The PatentsView API is intended to inspire the exploration and enhanced understanding of US intellectual property (IP) and innovation systems. We hope researchers and developers alike will explore the API to discover people and companies and to visualize trends and patterns across the US innovation enterprise.

The API contains 7 unique endpoints that users can query to tell new stories such as:

  1. Which companies are getting patents in 3D printing and where are they located? What were they patenting in before they started working on 3D printing?
  2. What technology has been most commonly patented in the US in the last 5 years? What are the top US cities for those patents? What are the top non-US cities for those patents?
  3. Who are the most prolific US inventors in the last 3 decades? How has their patenting activity (number of patents and types of patents) changed over time?
